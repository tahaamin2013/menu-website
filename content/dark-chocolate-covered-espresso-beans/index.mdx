## Dark Chocolate Covered Espresso Beans 
  
Love coffee? Love chocolate? Starbucks' Dark Chocolate Covered Espresso Beans are more than a snack. They're a yummy snack that gives you a quick energy boost. You'll love these crunchy snacks!

> Making these crunchy snacks is simple:
> 1. Pick the best coffee beans
> 2. Roast the beans to bring out their flavor
> 3. Cover each bean in smooth, dark chocolate
> 4. Let the chocolate harden
>
> The result? A crunchy bean inside, wrapped in creamy chocolate. It's the perfect mix of coffee kick and a sweet treat. Try some today!
