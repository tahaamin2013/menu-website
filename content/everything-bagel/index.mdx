Love bagels? You've got to try the Everything Bagel from Starbucks! It's a yummy choice that won't break the bank. Let's dive into what makes this bagel so special.

Anytime is a good time for a bagel! Try it:
- In the morning, with your coffee
- For a midday snack
- As a light dinner when you're in a hurry

The Everything Bagel from Starbucks is great for:
- Breakfast on the go
- A quick lunch
- An anytime snack
Vegans and vegetarians (without butter or cream cheese)

The Starbucks Everything Bagel is a soft, chewy New York-style bagel. It's topped with a mix of tasty seeds and spices:
- Onion
- Garlic
- Salt
- Poppy seeds
- Sesame seeds

### Ingredients

| Ingredient              | Components                                   | Sub-components  |
|-------------------------|----------------------------------------------|-----------------|
|UNBLEACHED ENRICHED FLOUR| WHEAT FLOUR, MALTED BARLEY FLOUR, NIACIN, REDUCED IRON, THIAMINE MONONITRATE, RIBOFLAVIN, FOLIC ACID                                |                 |
|WATER, SUGAR, SESAME SEEDS, CONTAIN 2% OR LESS OF: DEHYDRATED ONION, DEHYDRATED GARLIC, SALT, WHEAT GLUTEN, YEAST, TOASTED CHOPPED ONION, POPPY SEEDS, DISTILLED VINEGAR, GUAR GUM, ASCORBIC ACID, ENZYMES, MONOGLYCERIDES.           |                                 |                 |

You can get it with cream cheese or butter. Yum!
At Starbucks, you can customize your bagel:
- Get it warmed up
- Have it not warmed
- Add cream cheese or butter
- Enjoy it plain

Watching what you eat? Here's the scoop on calories in Starbucks bagels:
- The Everything Bagel has about 290 calories
- It has 5g of sugar
- There's 2.5g of fat

What is the cost of a Starbucks bagel? Good news! The Everything Bagel Starbucks price is reasonable. While prices can change, it's usually one of the more affordable items on the menu.

Ready to taste this delicious treat? Head to your local Starbucks and ask for an Everything Bagel. With its great taste and reasonable Starbucks bagel price, it's sure to become a new favorite!
Remember, whether you're counting calories in Starbucks bagels or just looking for a tasty snack, the Everything Bagel is a great choice. So why wait? Try one today!
