## Espresso Macchiato

  If you like strong coffee marked with just a spot of milk, you might love Espresso Macchiato. The name "macchiato" means "marked" in Italian. This drink comes from Europe. It's a classic that many coffee lovers enjoy.

> The standard Espresso Macchiato has three parts:
> 1. There's strong signature espresso roast coffee.
> 2. They add a bit of 2% steamed hot milk.
> 3. They put a thin layer of milk foam on top.

That's how they make the classic Espresso Macchiato. It's a small drink with big flavor.

The Signature Espresso Roast is bold, smooth, and has a caramel flavour. The standard number of espresso shots depends on the size of the drink:
