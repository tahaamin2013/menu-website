## Flat White 
  
Are you new to Coffee? Try a Flat White. It's an excellent starting point for exploring coffee drinks. A Flat White has less milk and more coffee taste than a latte.

Flat White is also a great option if you are a Cappuccino fan looking for a change. It's less foamy than a cappuccino.

Flat Whites are from Australia or New Zealand. Coffee lovers like them because they're not too strong or milky—just right!

A Flat White is a coffee drink that's popular in coffee shops. It's made like this:
1. They start with two Ristretto shots of signature espresso roast. 
2. Then, they add steamed hot whole milk.

That's how they make a not-too-strong, not-too-creamy, just-right flavor. 

"Ristretto" is Italian for "restricted" or "narrow." It uses less water than a regular espresso shot. The standard number of ristretto espresso shots depends on the size of the drink:

| Size    |   Volume     | Standard ristretto Espresso Shots |
|:--------|:-------------|:----------------------------------|
| Short   | 8 fl oz      | 2                                 |
| Tall    | 12 fl oz     | 2                                 |
| Grande  | 16 fl oz     | 3                                 | 
| Venti   | 20 fl oz     | 3                                 |
