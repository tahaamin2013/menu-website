## Starbucks Summer-Berry Starbucks Refreshers® Beverage 

Let's dive into the cool world of Starbucks' Summer-Berry Refreshers! This fruity drink is perfect for those hot summer days when you need a refreshing pick-me-up.

When to Enjoy a Summer Berry Refresher

This drink is perfect for:
- Cooling down on a hot day
- A fruity afternoon pick-me-up
- Enjoying with friends at the park
- Sipping while you work or study

The Summer-Berry Refresher is a tasty mix of raspberry, blueberry, and blackberry flavors. It's shaken with ice and poured over fun raspberry-flavored pearls. These little pearls burst into your mouth, adding an extra pop of flavor!

If you're watching your calories, you'll be happy to know that a Tall 12 fl oz Summer-Berry Refresher has only 70 calories. It also has 16g of sugar and no fat. While it's not exactly a health drink, it's a lighter choice compared to many other sweet beverages.

The Summer-Berry Refresher contains green coffee extract, which contains some caffeine. It's not as much as a regular coffee, but it's enough to give you a little energy boost.

You can customize your drink at Starbucks. Want more ice? Ask for extra. Prefer less? Go for light ice. You can even ask for no ice if you like. Plus, you can adjust how many scoops of those yummy raspberry pearls you get - from 1 to 12!

Next time you're at Starbucks, why not give the Summer-Berry Refresher a try? It's a burst of summer in every sip! Whether you're a fan of berries or just looking for something new, this drink is sure to please.

Remember, Starbucks offers lots of Refresher flavors. But for berry lovers, the Summer-Berry Refresher is hard to beat. It's like a little taste of summer, no matter what time of year it is!
