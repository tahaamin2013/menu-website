## Summer Skies Drink Starbucks Refreshers® Beverage

Looking for a cool drink to beat the heat? Starbucks has you covered with their new Summer Skies Refreshers beverage. This tasty treat is perfect for anyone who loves fruity, refreshing drinks on a hot day.

This Starbucks Refreshers drink is perfect for:
- Cooling off on hot summer days
- Treating yourself to a sweet, fruity drink
- Enjoying a light caffeine boost in the afternoon
- Trying something new and fun at Starbucks

The Summer Skies Refreshers is a sweet blend of berry flavors. It mixes raspberry, blueberry, and blackberry tastes for a burst of summer fun. The drink is shaken with ice and creamy coconut milk, then poured over raspberry-flavored pearls. These little pearls pop in your mouth for an extra sweet surprise!

You can customize your Summer Skies drink:
- Change the milk: Try almond milk, oat milk, or regular dairy options
- Adjust the ice: Ask for extra ice on really hot days, or go light on cooler ones
- Add more pearls: You can get up to 12 scoops of those yummy raspberry pearls!

A tall (12 fl oz) Summer Skies Refreshers has about 100 calories. It also has 19g of sugar and 2g of fat, making it a lighter option compared to many other sweet drinks.
