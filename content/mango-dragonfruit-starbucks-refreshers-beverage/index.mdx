Customize your drink to your liking. Start by choosing your preferred ice level: no ice, light ice, or extra ice. Next, select the number of Dragonfruit Inclusion scoops, ranging from 1 to 12.

Regarding mango dragon fruit refresher nutrition, it's a relatively low-calorie option. A Grande size contains 90 calories, and a tall size has even fewer at 70 calories. While this drink is a delicious treat, it does contain sugar. If you're watching your sugar intake, consider customizing it to reduce the sweetness.
