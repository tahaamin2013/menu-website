## Caramel Ribbon Crunch Frappuccino® Blended Beverage 

Love caramel? Crazy for coffee? The Caramel Ribbon Crunch Frappuccino at Starbucks might be your new favourite drink! Let's dive into this sweet, icy beverage.

This sweet treat is perfect for:
- Hot summer days
- Dessert cravings
- Coffee breaks with friends
- Anytime you need a caramel fix!

This frosty drink is packed with flavour. It starts with coffee, milk, and ice blended with buttery caramel syrup. Then comes the fun part - it's topped with:
- Dark caramel sauce
- Whipped cream
- More caramel drizzle
- Crunchy caramel-sugar topping
It's a caramel lover's dream come true!

The best part? You can customize your Caramel Ribbon Crunch Frappuccino:
1. Choose your milk: From nonfat to oat milk
2. Adjust the coffee: 1 to 12 pumps of Frappuccino Roast
3. More or less caramel: Change the dark caramel sauce pumps
4. Tweak the toppings: Extra crunch? Less whip? You decide!

This drink is tasty, but it's not light on calories. A Tall (12 oz) has:
- 330 calories
- 42g sugar
- 15g fat

The Caramel Ribbon Crunch Frappuccino has Caffeine in it, so you'll get a little pick-me-up. The exact amount of Caffeine can vary based on the size and how you customize it.

While the Caramel Ribbon Crunch is usually served cold, you can ask for a hot caramel drink with similar flavours. Just chat with your barista!

Head to your local Starbucks and ask for a Caramel Ribbon Crunch Frappuccino. Remember, you can always adjust the sweetness or toppings to make it perfect for you.

So, next time you're at Starbucks, why not try this caramel-packed, crunchy, creamy drink? It might just become your new go-to indulgence!
