Ever wondered what those shell-shaped treats at Starbucks are? They're madeleines, and they're taking the coffee world by storm! These French cakes are not just delicious; they're also packed with history and culture.

Madeleines are small, buttery cakes from France. They're soft and moist inside, with crispy edges. Starbucks makes them with top-notch ingredients, giving you a taste of France right in your neighbourhood!

Who can enjoy madeleines? Anyone with a sweet tooth! They're perfect for:
- Coffee lovers looking for a tasty snack
- People who want a quick breakfast on the go
- Anyone craving a light dessert

Madeleines are great any time of day:
- With your morning coffee
- As an afternoon pick-me-up
- For a late-night treat

Starbucks madeleines are made with simple, quality ingredients:
- Flour
- Sugar
- Eggs
- Butter

But watch out! Each madeleine has 220 calories, 17g of sugar, and 11g of fat. Enjoy in moderation!

Did you know? Madeleines aren't just tasty treats. They've made their mark in other ways:
- Madeleine McCann: A famous missing person case that captivated the world
- Madeleine Stowe: A talented actress known for her roles in "Revenge" and "12 Monkeys"
- Madeleine Albright: The first woman to become United States Secretary of State

Here are some interesting tidbits about madeleines and people named Madeleine:
- Madeleine McGraw is a young actress making waves in Hollywood
- Some fans wonder if Madeleine Cookie and Espresso Cookie are dating in the game "Cookie Run."
- Swedish actress Madeleine Martin has captured hearts with her performances

Next time you're at Starbucks, why not give Madeleines a try? They're a delicious way to add a touch of French elegance to your day. Pair them with your favourite coffee and enjoy a little taste of France; no passport is required!

Remember, whether you're a fan of madeleines or just curious about them, there's always something new to learn about these delightful treats. So go ahead, take a bite, and discover the magic of madeleines for yourself!
