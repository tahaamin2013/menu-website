## Iced Passion Tango® Tea 

  Looking for a caffeine-free, cool, refreshing drink bursting with flavor? Enter the world of Starbucks' Iced Passion Tango® Tea. It's not a drink; it's a vibrant, passionate journey for your taste buds!

  Iced Passion Tango® Tea is great any time! Hot day? It cools you down fast. Need a boost? It perks you up. Having brunch? Share it with friends. Want to relax? It's caffeine-free. This tea fits every moment!

  Whether you're a tea lover or a health-conscious sipper, this drink covers you. It's great for all ages and tastes, making it a crowd-pleaser at any gathering.

  Hibiscus makes it tart and red. Lemongrass adds a citrus taste. Apple makes it a bit sweet. All parts are caffeine-free. You can drink it any time. It comes with ice.

  You can change your tea! Here's how:

  Water options:
  - Extra water
  - No water
  - Light water

  Ice options:
  - Extra ice
  - No ice
  - Light ice

  Pick what you like best!

  While Iced Passion Tango® Tea is delicious, its vibrant red color can stain clothes. Sip carefully to avoid any mishaps!

  0 calories, 0g sugar, 0g fat
