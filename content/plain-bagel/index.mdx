Are you a bagel lover? Let's talk about the classic plain bagel! This simple yet delicious bread is a favorite for many. Whether you're grabbing breakfast on the go or looking for a quick snack, plain bagels are a great choice.

A plain bagel is a round bread with a hole in the middle. It's chewy on the inside and slightly crispy on the outside. Many people think of New York when they think of bagels!

Plain bagels are great any time:
- For breakfast with cream cheese
- As a lunch sandwich base
- For a quick snack
- Toasted with butter for a treat

Plain bagels are simple. They're usually made with:
- Flour
- Water
- Sugar
- Salt
- Yeast

### Ingredients

| Ingredient              | Components                                   | Sub-components  |
|-------------------------|----------------------------------------------|-----------------|

| Water, Sugar, Contains 2% Or Less Of: Wheat Gluten, Salt, Yeast, Distilled Vinegar, Guar Gum, Ascorbic Acid, Microbial Enzymes, Monoglycerides.                  |                                  |                 |

At Starbucks, you can customize your bagel:
- Get it warmed up
- Have it not warmed

People often ask, "How many calories is a plain bagel?" Here's what you need to know:
- A typical plain bagel has about 290 calories
- It contains around 6g of sugar
- There's only about 1g of fat in a plain bagel
