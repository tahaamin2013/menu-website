Let's talk about Starbucks Iced Green Tea! This cool drink is perfect for anyone looking for a refreshing and healthy sip.

This drink is great for:
- A cool morning pick-me-up
- A refreshing afternoon break
- A healthy alternative to sugary drinks
- Hydrating on a hot day

Starbucks Iced Green Tea is a simple but tasty blend. It starts with green tea, then adds mint, lemongrass, and lemon verbena. They shake it all up with ice to make it extra refreshing!

At Starbucks, you can customize your drink:
- Want it stronger? Ask for light water.
- Like it milder? Go for extra water.
- Love ice? Get extra ice.
- Prefer less ice? Ask for light ice.

You can make it the way you like!
Good news for calorie counters! Starbucks Iced Green Tea has:
- 0 calories, 0g sugar, 0g fat
It's a great choice if you're watching what you eat or drink.

There's caffeine in this drink! Green tea naturally has some caffeine, but more than coffee. It's enough to give you a little energy boost without making you jittery.

Iced Green Tea is one of the green tea options at Starbucks. Check out their menu for hot green tea and other green tea-based drinks too!

Next time you're at Starbucks, why not try the Iced Green tea? It's a tasty way to get some of the health benefits of green tea in a refreshing iced drink. Give it a sip and see why so many people love this cool, green treat!
