## Iced Brown Sugar Oatmilk Shaken Espresso 
Looking for a refreshing iced drink full of energy? Try Iced Brown Sugar Oat milk Shaken Espresso. It gives you a cool, sweet kick. 

It's very similar to Iced Shaken Espresso. It is particularly for those who like plant-based milk with cinnamon and brown sugar flavours.

> How it's made at Starbucks:
> 1. Blend Espresso Roast shots shake well with cinnamon powder and brown sugar syrup pumps.
> 2. Pour into a cup with ice
> 3. Top with oatmilk

The standard Blend Espresso Roast shots and brown sugar syrup pumps depends on the size of the drink:
