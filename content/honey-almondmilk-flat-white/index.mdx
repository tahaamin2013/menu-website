## Honey Almondmilk Flat White 

Are you looking for a tasty, light coffee drink? The Starbucks Honey Almondmilk Flat White might be what you need! This drink is perfect for anyone who loves a hint of sweetness in their coffee.

This drink is great for:
- A morning pick-me-up
- An afternoon treat
- Any time you want a light but tasty coffee drink

The Starbucks Honey Almondmilk Flat White is a simple but yummy drink. Here's what's in it:
- Almondmilk
- Starbucks® Blonde Espresso Roast
- A touch of honey


### Ingredients
| Ingredient              | Components                                   | Sub-components  |
|-------------------------|----------------------------------------------|-----------------|
| ALMONDMILK              |FILTERED WATER, ALMONDS, SUGAR, CALCIUM CARBONATE, SUNFLOWER LECITHIN, SEA SALT, XANTHAN GUM, GUAR GUM, VITAMIN A PALMITATE, VITAMIN D2 (ERGOCALCIFEROL)   |                 |
| BREWED ESPRESSO         |                                               |                 |
| HONEY BLEND             | HONEY, WATER, NATURAL FLAVORS, POTASSIUM SORBATE, XANTHAN GUM, CITRIC ACID                                              |                 |

This mix creates a drink that's creamy, nutty, and just a little bit sweet.

Watching your calories? Here's what you need to know about the Starbucks Honey Almond Flat White calories:
- A small (8 oz) serving has 80 calories
- It contains 12g of sugar
- There's 2.5g of fat
