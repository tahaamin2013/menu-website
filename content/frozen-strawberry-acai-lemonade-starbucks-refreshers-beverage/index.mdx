## Frozen Strawberry Açaí Lemonade Starbucks Refreshers® Beverage 

Are you looking for a cool, fruity drink to enjoy on a hot day? Beat the Heat with Starbucks' Frozen Strawberry Açaí Lemonade Refresher. 

It's a tasty blend that'll make you feel like you're on a tropical vacation! The mix of strawberry and açaí with tangy lemonade is super tasty.
Plus, the slushy texture makes it extra refreshing on a hot day.

This drink is perfect for:
- Hot summer days
- After a workout
- Beach trips
- Anytime you need a fruity pick-me-up!

The Frozen Strawberry Açaí Lemonade Refresher is packed with flavor.

Here's what's inside:
- Sweet strawberry flavours
- Tropical açaí notes
- Real strawberry pieces
- Strawberry puree
- Tangy lemonade
- Ice (to make it nice and frosty!)

All these ingredients are blended to create a refreshing, slushy-like drink.

Want to change things up? No problem! You can:
- Ask for extra strawberry puree (or less, or none at all)
- Add more strawberries (up to 12 scoops!)
- Get extra lemonade, less lemonade, or no lemonade

If you're counting calories, here's what you need to know:
- A  Tall 12 fl oz Frozen Strawberry Açaí Lemonade Refresher has about 120 calories
- It contains 26g of sugar
- There's 0g of fat

The calorie count can change based on the size you order:
- Grande Strawberry Açaí Lemonade: around 160 calories
- Venti Strawberry Açaí Lemonade: about 210 calories
- Trenta Strawberry Açaí Refresher: approximately 250 calories
