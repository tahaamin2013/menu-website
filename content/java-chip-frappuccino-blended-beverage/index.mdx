## Java Chip Frappuccino® Blended Beverage 

"Java" is a slang term for coffee. It comes from the island of Java in Indonesia, which was once a major coffee producer.

> Java Chip Frappuccino® Blended Beverage is made for coffee lovers obsessed with sweets. It's perfect if you want a dessert-like drink that still gives you a caffeine kick.

You can drink it anytime; you need a pick-me-up! It's great for hot summer days, as an afternoon treat, or when you're out with friends.

> The standard Java Chip Frappuccino has:
> 1. Frappuccino Roast, whole milk, and ice blended with mocha sauce and Frappuccino chips.
> 2. Whipped cream on top with mocha drizzle.

The standard number of Frappuccino Roast pumps, mocha sauce pumps, and Frappuccino chips varies depending on the drink size.


    | Standard Recipe                   | Frappuccino Roast pumps   | Mocha Sauce pumps                   | Frappuccino chips|
    |:----------------------------------|:--------------------------|:------------------------------------|:-----------------|
    | Tall                              | 2                         | 2                                   | 2                |
    | Grande                            | 3                         | 3                                   |  3               |
    | Venti                             | 4                         | 4                                   |  4               |

Want to change things up? You can! Try:
- Adjusting the number of Frappuccino Roast pumps, mocha sauce pumps, and Frappuccino chips (0-12).
- Customising the whipped cream: none, light, regular, or extra.
- Selecting alternative milk options: 
   - Standard options: Non-fat, 2%, or whole milk
   - Dairy alternatives: Almond, coconut, oat, or soy milk
   - Creamy choices: Breve (half & half), heavy cream, or sweet cream
   - Special option: Non-dairy vanilla sweet cream
- Modifying the mocha drizzle: standard, extra mocha drizzle, light mocha drizzle, or no mocha drizzle.

> One tall 12 fl oz cup has 320 calories, 42g sugar, and 14g fat.
