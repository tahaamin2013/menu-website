## Mocha Cookie Crumble Frappuccino® 
  
Looking for Sweet and Creamy cold coffee on hot days? Try Mocha Cookie Crumble Frappuccino®, a perfect delight of coffee and chocolate.

The Mocha Cookie Crumble Frappuccino® has many yummy parts:

1. It starts with Frappuccino® Roast coffee pumps.
2. Mocha sauce pumps add rich chocolate flavour.
3. Frappuccino® chips give it extra texture.
4. Whole Milk and ice make the drink cold and smooth.
5. Whipped cream is layered inside and on top.
6. Chocolate cookie pieces add crunch.
7. A final touch of chocolate Mocha drizzles on top.

The number of Frappuccino® Roast pumps, Mocha sauce pumps, and Frappuccino® chips varies depending on the drink size.

<div style={{ overflowX: 'scroll' }}>
| Size   | Frappuccino® Roast pumps | Mocha sauce pumps | Frappuccino® chips |
|:-------|:------------------------:|:-----------------:|:------------------:|
| Tall   | 2                        | 2                 | 2                  |
| Grande | 3                        | 3                 | 3                  |
| Venti  | 4                        | 4                 | 4                  |
</div>
All these parts blend to make a tasty, refreshing drink. Each sip is full of flavor from top to bottom.
