Are you looking for a tasty, energizing drink to start your day? The Starbucks Iced Hazelnut Oatmilk Shaken Espresso might be what you need! This yummy coffee treat combines the rich taste of espresso with the nutty sweetness of hazelnut and the creamy goodness of oat milk.

The Iced Hazelnut Oatmilk Shaken Espresso is a mix of:
- Starbucks Blonde Espresso (or regular espresso if Blonde isn't available)
- Hazelnut syrup for that nutty flavor
- Ice to keep it cool and refreshing
- Oat milk for a creamy, plant-based finish
All these ingredients are shaken together to create a perfect blend of flavors in every sip!

Want to change things up? You can customize your drink:
- Try different milk options like almond, coconut, or soy
- Add more or less hazelnut syrup (1 to 12 pumps)
- Choose your espresso type (Blonde, Signature, or Decaf)
- Pick how many espresso shots you want (1 to 12)

This coffee is great for:
- People who like their coffee with a hint of nutty sweetness
- Folks looking for a dairy-free option (thanks to the oat milk!)
Anyone who needs a pick-me-up during the day

The Iced Hazelnut Oatmilk Shaken Espresso is perfect for:
- Mornings when you need an extra boost
- Afternoon breaks to help you power through the rest of your day
- Hot summer days when you want something cool and refreshing

If you're counting calories, a Tall (12 fl oz) has about 100 calories, 8g of sugar, and 3.5g of fat. Not bad for a tasty treat!
