    Coffee lovers favour Starbucks Espresso for its robust and bold flavours. It comes without added creams or sugars. 

    Starbucks Espresso provides a mild caffeine boost to start your day. It is also a quick energy boost during busy afternoons for students and late-night work.

    The standard recipe includes Signature Espresso Roast. The Signature Espresso Roast is bold, smooth, and has a caramel flavour. The standard number of espresso shots depends on the size of the drink:

    <div style={{width:"300px"}}>
    | Size    |              | Standard Espresso Shots |
    |:--------|:-------------|:------------------------|
    | Solo    | 0.75 fl oz   | 1                       |
    | Doppio  | 1.5 fl oz    | 2                       |
    | Triple  | 2.25 fl oz   | 3                       |
    | Quad    | 3 fl oz      | 4                       |
    </div>

    If you like a smooth, vibrant, and slightly citrusy taste, you can customise your drink by choosing Blonde Espresso Roast.

    If you don't like Signature or Blonde Espresso Roast, you can choose one of the following:

    - ⅓ Decaf Espresso Roast
    - ½ Decaf Espresso Roast
    - ⅔ Decaf Espresso Roast
    - Decaf Espresso Roast

    You can also customise the number of shots for any size, up to a maximum of 12 shots.


    ### Starbucks Espresso Price

    Starbucks offers a variety of coffee cup sizes, the prices are as follows:

    <div style={{width:"300px"}}>
    | Size    |   Volume     | Price |
    |:--------|:-------------|:------|
    | Solo    | 0.7 fl oz    | $2.45 |
    | Doppio  | 1.5 fl oz    | $2.95 |
    | Triple  | 2.25 fl oz   | $3.45 |
    | Quad    | 3 fl oz      | $3.95 |
    </div>

    These prices apply particularly to the United States and are for the year 2024.
