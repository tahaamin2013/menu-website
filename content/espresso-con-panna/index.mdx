## Espresso Con Panna
  
Starbucks Espresso Con Panna combines fresh whipped Cream topping with espresso. The topping improves the espresso's simple yet rich flavour.
Espresso's caramel taste is very good for bold coffee lovers. But if you have a sweet tooth, adding the whipped cream makes this caramel taste more delightful. The drink is an ideal luxurious yet simple coffee experience.

The standard recipe includes Signature Espresso Roast as well as whipped Cream topping. The Signature Espresso Roast is bold, smooth, and has a caramel flavour. The standard number of espresso shots depends on the size of the drink:

<div style={{width:"300px"}}>
| Size    |  Volume      | Standard Espresso Shots |
|:--------|:-------------|:------------------------|
| Solo    | 0.75 fl oz   | 1                       |
| Doppio  | 1.5 fl oz    | 2                       |
| Triple  | 2.25 fl oz   | 3                       |
| Quad    | 3 fl oz      | 4                       |
</div>

If you like a smooth, vibrant, and slightly citrusy taste, you can customise your drink by choosing Blonde Espresso Roast.

If you don't like Signature or Blonde Espresso Roast, you can choose one of the following:

- ⅓ Decaf Espresso Roast
- ½ Decaf Espresso Roast
- ⅔ Decaf Espresso Roast
- Decaf Espresso Roast

You can also customise the number of shots for any size, up to a maximum of 12 shots.

Whether you prefer your drink with no whipped cream at all, a light touch, or extra topping, the choice is yours.
