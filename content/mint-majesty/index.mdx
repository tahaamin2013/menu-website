Are you looking for a cool, refreshing drink without the buzz? Mint Majesty tea might be what you need! This tasty herbal tea from Teavana, now available at Starbucks, is perfect for any time of day.

Mint Majesty is a caffeine-free herbal tea that blends mint with a hint of lemon verbena. It's super refreshing and great for cooling down on a hot day or relaxing in the evening.

If you're wondering, "Does Mint Majesty have caffeine?" or "Is Mint Majesty caffeine-free?" - the answer is clear. Starbucks Mint Majesty tea is 100% caffeine-free. So, you can enjoy it any time without worrying about staying up all night.

Starbucks Mint Majesty tea is simple and natural. It contains:
- Water
- Spearmint leaves
- Peppermint leaves
- Lemon verbena
