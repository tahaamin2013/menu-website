## Impossible Breakfast Sandwich 
  
Craving a hearty meal that's both meatless and protein-packed? Look no further! The Starbucks Impossible™ Breakfast Sandwich is here to rock your world.

It is a versatile option for any time of the day. It's a delicious choice for breakfast or whenever you're feeling hungry! It's a Game-Changer for Vegetarians and meat lovers alike!

> This sandwich has:
> - A sesame ciabatta bun
> - A fried egg from cage-free hens
> - Impossible™ Sausage Made from Plants
> - Sharp Cheddar cheese

The plant-based sausage, with its meaty flavors, is a unique and intriguing choice for those who want to eat less meat but still enjoy the taste of it.
