Ever wondered what's in a cinnamon dolce latte? Let's dive into this sweet Starbucks favorite!
It's a warm hug in a cup! The Starbucks cinnamon dolce latte mixes:
- Rich espresso
- Steamed milk
- Sweet cinnamon dolce syrup
- Topped with whipped cream and cinnamon sprinkles

### Ingredients
| Ingredient              | Components                                   |
|-------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| MILK, BREWED ESPRESSO   |                                              |
| CINNAMON DOLCE SYRUP    | SUGAR, WATER, NATURAL FLAVOR, CITRIC ACID, POTASSIUM SORBATE |
| WHIPPED CREAM            | CREAM (CREAM, MONO AND DIGLYCERIDES, CARAGEENAN) |
| VANILLA SYRUP           | SUGAR, WATER, NATURAL FLAVORS, POTASSIUM SORBATE, CITRIC ACID |
| CINNAMON DOLCE TOPPING  | SUGAR, CINNAMON, SALT, EXTRACTIVES OF BUTTER (MILK), AND OTHER NATURAL FLAVOR |

At Starbucks, you can customize your cinnamon dolce latte:
- Milk: Try almond, oat, or coconut milk
- Espresso: Choose blonde or decaf
- Syrup: Adjust sweetness (0-12 pumps)
- Toppings: Extra or no whipped cream and sprinkles

Yum! But what if you want to make a cinnamon dolce latte at home? Keep reading!
- Brew strong espresso
- Heat milk (any kind works!)
- Add cinnamon dolce syrup
- Mix it all up
- Top with whipped cream and cinnamon

Want an iced cinnamon dolce latte? Just use cold milk and pour over ice!

A grande (16 oz) has 340 calories and 40g sugar. But you can make it lighter:
- Skip the whipped cream
- Use sugar-free syrup
- Choose a low-fat milk
