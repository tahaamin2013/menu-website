## Summer-Berry Lemonade Starbucks Refreshers® Beverage

Summer-Berry Lemonade Refresher is a tasty blend of berry flavours and tangy lemonade.
If you love fruity drinks with a bit of zing, you'll enjoy this Refresher.

This drink is perfect for:
- Hot summer days
- Picnics in the park
- Beach trips
- Anytime you need a fruity pick-me-up!

The Summer-Berry Lemonade Refresher is packed with flavor. It mixes raspberry, blueberry, and blackberry tastes with Starbucks' classic lemonade.
The best part? It comes with little raspberry-flavoured pearls that burst in your mouth! Those little bursting pearls make it fun to drink!

Here's what goes into this refreshing drink:
- Ice
- Summer berry base (with natural flavours and a touch of coffee extract)
- Lemonade
- Raspberry-flavored pearls

Want to change things up? No problem! You can:
- Add more raspberry pearls (up to 12 scoops)
- Ask for more or less ice
- Get extra lemonade, less lemonade, or no lemonade at all
If you're counting calories, here's what you need to know about a Tall 12 fl oz Summer-Berry Lemonade Refresher:
- 110 calories
- 0 grams of fat
- 26 grams of carbs (25 grams are sugars)
- 30 mg of sodium
- 35 mg of caffeine
