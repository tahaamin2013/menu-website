Love coffee? Need a tasty pick-me-up any time of day? Starbucks Pike Place Roast might be your new best friend!

This smooth, medium-roast coffee has been a Starbucks star since 2008. It's named after the famous Pike Place Market in Seattle, where Starbucks started.

What makes Pike Place Roast special?
- It's always fresh: Starbucks brews it all day long in their stores.
- It's well-rounded: Not too strong, not too weak - just right!
- It has yummy flavors: Think hints of chocolate and toasted nuts.
- It's made from top-notch Latin American coffee beans.

When to drink Pike Place Roast:
- Morning wake-up call? Yes!
- Afternoon energy boost? You bet!
- Any time you want a reliable, tasty coffee.

**Good news for calorie counters: **A big 16-ounce cup (that's a "Grande" in Starbucks-speak) has only 5 calories. There is no sugar or fat—just pure coffee goodness!
Want to try Pike Place Roast?
- Visit your local Starbucks
- Buy beans to brew at home
- Ask for it hot or iced
