## Oleato™ Caffé Latte with Oatmilk 

Have you heard about the new Oleato™ Caffé Latte with Oatmilk at Starbucks? It's a drink that's got people talking! Let's take a closer look at this unique coffee treat.

This new latte is made with:
- Starbucks Blonde Espresso infused with Partanna® extra virgin olive oil
- Steamed oatmilk

The result? A smooth, rich drink that's both familiar and new.

> **Note:** If Starbucks® Blonde Espresso Roast is not available, Signature Espresso Roast will be substituted.

Detailed Ingredients:
The Oleato Caffé Latte with Oatmilk could be great for:
- Coffee lovers looking for something new
- People who enjoy plant-based milk options
- Anyone curious about the olive oil and coffee combo

- Change the milk foam amount
- Adjust the milk temperature
