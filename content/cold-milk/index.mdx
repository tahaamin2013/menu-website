## Can babies drink cold milk?
Yes, babies can drink cold milk. Even newborns can drink cold breast milk. But remember, Starbucks milk isn't for babies. Always ask your doctor about what's best for your little one.
## Is cold milk good when you have a cold?
Some people think milk causes more mucus when you have a cold. But there's no strong proof of this. If cold milk makes you feel good when you're sick, it's okay to drink it.
