Looking for a cool, creamy drink to beat the heat? Try the Starbucks White Chocolate Macadamia Cream Cold Brew! This yummy new drink is perfect for coffee lovers who want something sweet and refreshing.

The White Chocolate Macadamia Cream Cold Brew is great for:
- Hot summer days
- Afternoon pick-me-ups
- Coffee dates with friends
- Treating yourself after a long day

This drink starts with Starbucks' famous cold brew coffee. Then, they add:
- Macadamia syrup for nutty sweetness
- White chocolate macadamia cream cold foam on top
- Crunchy toasted cookie bits as a finishing touch
It's like a dessert in a cup!

You can customize your drink:
- Want it colder? Ask for extra ice.
- Prefer less ice? Request light ice.
- No ice at all? They can do that, too!

A 12 oz (Tall) serving has:
- 160 calories
- 19g of sugar
- 9g of fat
If you're counting calories, consider enjoying this drink as an occasional treat.

While the cold brew itself is gluten-free, the cookie topping might contain gluten. If you have dietary restrictions, ask your barista for more info.

Love this drink so much you want to try making it yourself? Here's a simple way to recreate it:
1. Start with cold-brew coffee
2. Add a splash of macadamia nut syrup
3. Top with whipped cream mixed with white chocolate sauce
4. Sprinkle with crushed macadamia nuts

It won't be the same, but it'll be close!

Starbucks' White Chocolate Macadamia Cream Cold Brew is a sweet, nutty twist on regular iced coffee. It's perfect for those who love creamy, indulgent drinks. Give it a try and see what you think!
