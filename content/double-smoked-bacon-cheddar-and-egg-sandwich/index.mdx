## Double-Smoked Bacon, Cheddar & Egg Sandwich 

Are you looking for a quick and filling breakfast? The Starbucks Double-Smoked Bacon, Cheddar & Egg Sandwich might be what you need! This tasty sandwich is packed with flavor and protein, making it a popular choice for busy mornings.

This sandwich is perfect for:
- A quick breakfast on the go
- A satisfying lunch option
- An afternoon pick-me-up
Why Choose the Starbucks Double Bacon Sandwich?
1. High in protein to keep you full
2. Made with quality ingredients like cage-free eggs
3. Features double-smoked bacon for extra flavor
4. Served on a flaky croissant bun
The Starbucks Double-Smoked Bacon Sandwich is a real treat. Here's what you'll find inside:
- Bacon smoked for six hours over hickory wood chips
- A cage-free fried egg
- A melted slice of sharp Cheddar cheese
- All served on Starbucks' signature croissant bun
