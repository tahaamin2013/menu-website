## Cheese Danish

Let's talk about the yummy Starbucks Cheese Danish! This tasty treat is a favorite for many coffee lovers. It's the perfect mix of flaky and cheesy goodness.

This tasty treat is great for:
- A quick breakfast on the go
- An afternoon snack with your coffee
- A light lunch when you're in a hurry

The Starbucks Cheese Danish is made with buttery croissant dough. Inside, you'll find soft, warm Neufchatel cheese. It's simple but so good!

At Starbucks, you can choose how you want your Danish:
- Warmed up for that fresh-from-the-oven taste
- Not warmed if you prefer it cool

Watching your diet? Here's what you need to know:
- A Starbucks Cheese Danish has 290 calories
- It contains 11g of sugar
- There's 14g of fat in each danish

If you're counting carbs, you'll want to know that a Starbucks Cheese Danish does have some. While it's not healthy food, it can be a nice treat now and then. It's got some protein from the cheese, which is good. If you're vegetarian, you're in luck - this Danish is veggie-friendly!

The Cheese Danish is a star on the Starbucks pastry menu. But remember, Starbucks offers other dishes, too, if you want to try something different.

Remember, while Starbucks is known for coffee, its food items, like the Cheese Danish, are pretty tasty, too. Give it a try and see why so many people love this flaky, cheesy treat! It's a classic treat that goes great with any drink.
