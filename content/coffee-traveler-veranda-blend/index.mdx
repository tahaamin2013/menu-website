Need coffee for a crowd? Meet the Starbucks Coffee Traveler - Veranda Blend!

This handy coffee carrier is perfect for your next big meeting, family picnic, or any time you need to caffeinate a group. It's packed with 96 ounces of Starbucks' light and tasty Blonde Veranda Blend. That's enough to fill 12 regular cups!

Why choose the Starbucks Coffee Traveler?
- It's convenient: No need to juggle multiple cups or make several coffee runs.
- It's crowd-pleasing: Veranda Blend is light and smooth - great for most coffee drinkers.
- It's fresh: You get that just-brewed Starbucks taste wherever you go.
- It's low-calorie: Only 5 calories per serving, with no sugar or fat!
