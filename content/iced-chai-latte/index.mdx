## Iced Chai Latte 

Looking for a cool drink on hot days? Try Starbucks Iced Chai Latte. It’s like a cold, spicy version of a [latte](https://starbucks-menu-with-prices.net/caffe-latte). An excellent option for those in search of coffee-free pick-me-up. It is great for cooling off. 

> The standard Iced Chai Latte has two parts:
> 1. Black tea pumps get mixed with spices like cinnamon and clove.
> 2. Then, they add 2% milk and ice.

The standard number of chai pumps depends on the size of the drink:

<div style={{width:"300px"}}>
| Size    |  Volume      | Standard chai pumps               |
|:--------|:-------------|:----------------------------------|
| Tall    | 12 fl oz     | 3                                 |
| Grande  | 16 fl oz     | 4                                 |
| Venti   | 20 fl oz     | 6                                 |
</div>

It’s sweet and spicy and tastes great when it's cold.
